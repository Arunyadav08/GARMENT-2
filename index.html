🧵 Our Story

Born from a Passion for Indian Craft

Elara Threads was founded in 2018 in Jaipur by a team of textile artisans and fashion enthusiasts who believed Indian craftsmanship deserved a global stage. We work directly with weavers, block printers, and embroiderers across Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, and Tamil Nadu.

Today we serve thousands of customers across 400+ cities in India — delivering authentically crafted, beautifully finished garments at honest prices.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also log into your account and visit "My Orders" to track in real time. We partner with Delhivery, BlueDart, and Ekart.

What is your return & refund policy?

We offer 10-day easy returns on all products. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 10 days of delivery. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. Note: customised/stitched items cannot be returned unless defective.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, EMI on orders above ₹3,000, and Cash on Delivery in select pin codes.

Do you offer custom stitching / tailoring?

Yes! Many of our products can be custom stitched to your measurements. Simply add your measurements at checkout or contact our team. Custom orders take 5–7 extra working days.

How can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placing them. Go to "My Orders" in your account or contact our support team. Once the order is shipped, cancellation is not possible, but you can initiate a return after delivery.

Do you deliver across all of India?

Yes! We deliver to 400+ cities across India. Metro cities get delivery in 1–2 days, Tier 2 cities in 3–4 days, and remote areas in 5–7 days. Enter your PIN code at checkout to see timelines.

Is Cash on Delivery available?

Yes, COD is available in most pin codes. A handling fee of ₹40 may apply. Check availability at checkout by entering your pin code.
