🧵 Our Story
Born from a Passion for Indian Craft

Elara Threads was founded in 2018 in Jaipur by a team of textile artisans and fashion enthusiasts who believed Indian craftsmanship deserved a global stage.

We work directly with weavers, block printers, and embroiderers across Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, and Tamil Nadu — delivering authentically crafted, beautifully finished garments at honest prices.

🌿

Natural Fabrics

Pure cottons, silks, and linens. No synthetics, no shortcuts.

🧶

Artisan Made

Every piece supports skilled craftspeople and preserves heritage techniques.

🇮🇳

Made in India

Proudly manufactured and shipped from Indian workshops.

♻️

Eco-Friendly

FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I track my order?＋
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also log into your account and visit "My Orders" to track in real time. We partner with Delhivery, BlueDart, and Ekart.
What is your return & refund policy?＋
We offer 10-day easy returns on all products. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. Note: customised/stitched items cannot be returned unless defective.
What payment methods do you accept?＋
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, EMI on orders above ₹3,000, and Cash on Delivery in select pin codes.
Do you offer custom stitching / tailoring?＋
Yes! Many of our products can be custom stitched to your measurements. Simply add your measurements at checkout or contact our team. Custom orders take 5–7 extra working days.
Do you deliver across all of India?＋
Yes! We deliver to 400+ cities across India. Metro cities get delivery in 1–2 days, Tier 2 cities in 3–4 days, and remote areas in 5–7 days.
Is Cash on Delivery available?＋
Yes, COD is available in most pin codes. A handling fee of ₹40 may apply. Check availability at checkout by entering your pin code.
